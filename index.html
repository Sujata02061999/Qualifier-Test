About Us

Archer Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd (Archer Pharma) is the fourth largest specialty generic pharmaceutical company in the world with global revenues of more than US$ 4.5 billion. Supported by more than 40 manufacturing facilities, we provide high-quality, affordable medicines, trusted by healthcare professionals and patients, to more than 100 countries across the globe
